About Us

A tiny studio building games that stay light, playful, and fair.

We are a very small team of two developers who enjoy creating simple games with clear ideas and a little personality. Our work is inspired by classics and quick-play puzzle games, but we try to keep each release feeling clean, original, and easy to enjoy.

You will usually find us making games like 2048-style puzzlers, color challenges, memory games, and number-based brain teasers. We care a lot about keeping things straightforward, accessible, and free from forced ad interruptions.
